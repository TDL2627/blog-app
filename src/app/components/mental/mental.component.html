Mental Health

Written By:
Zintle Meslane

There is a special need for mental and behavioural awareness in our communities and how individuals can actually seek the necessary help through counselling and medication. Taking care of your mental health is just as important as taking care of your physical health. Mental health determines how we handle stress, relate to others and make choices. Factors contributing to mental health problems include: Life experiences such as trauma, abuse or grief and loss, biological factors such as genes or brain chemistry, family history of mental health problems.
