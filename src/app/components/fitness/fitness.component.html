Fitness

Written By:
Mpumezo Theo Gqoboka

For me, fitness is first and foremost about feeling good and being able to move without pain. Fitness is such an important part of life, one should be physically as well as mentally fit. A healthy fit person can never be a liability on society. Being fit boosts energy, mood, sleep, and your immune system. Staying fit benefits long-term health in big ways. There are many fitness trends in South Africa like running clubs, group training, laughter yoga etc. Other South Africans opt for budget-friendly options like jogging or watching online videos. With something for every type of exerciser, these fitness trends mean that there are no more excuses for skipping out on your daily dose of exercise.
